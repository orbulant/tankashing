# EasyAccel

<img src="/easyaccel.png" alt="EasyAccel's OpenGraph Image" width="350" />

EasyAccel is founded in 2022 by a small group of friends believing in upscaling businesses through digitilization, all while being managed and done by EasyAccel, thus alleviating businesses from the hassle of creating an e-commerce.

**"Spend more time running the business, than thinking about the business."**

EasyAccel accelerates small Malaysian businesses to grow their online presence through an E-Commerce platformed powered by Shopify and NextJS/Vercel for a powerful technical advantage. We are dedicated in creating low-cost high impact solutions that is delivered with a quick time-to-market, thus giving our clients a competitive advantage in their respective industries.


Tech Used: NextJS, Vercel, Shopify, AstroJS, etc.

[Click here to visit EasyAccel!](https://www.easyaccel.com/)

# BigOrder

<img src="/bigorder.png" alt="BigOrder Login Page" width="350" />

A **Full-Stack (MERN)** web application for small-medium food establishments that allows customers to place orders online via the website after scanning a QR Code.

Tech Used: MongoDB, ExpressJS, ReactJS, NodeJS, Post Deployment Scripts, Git

[Click here to visit BigOrder hosted on Heroku!](https://bigorder.herokuapp.com/)
